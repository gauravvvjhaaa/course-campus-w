Introduction

Course Campus ("we", "our", "us") is an education consultancy located at 316/11 Shani Bazar, Bandh Road, Sangam Vihar, New Delhi 110080. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or contact us for educational guidance.

By submitting any form on this website or contacting us, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your information.

📥

Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us through our forms or contact channels. This may include:

  • Full Name — to personalise our communication with you
  • Phone Number — to call or WhatsApp you regarding your enquiry or admission
  • Email Address — to send you relevant information about courses and admissions (optional)
  • Course Interest — to understand which program you are interested in
  • Educational Qualification — to assess eligibility for programs
  • Message or Query — any additional information you choose to share
🔒 We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, passwords, or Aadhaar numbers through this website.
🎯

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for educational counseling and admission purposes. Specifically:

  • To contact you via phone or WhatsApp to provide free educational counseling
  • To assist you with course selection, admission forms, and enrollment guidance
  • To send you relevant information about programs, deadlines, and exam schedules
  • To respond to your queries and follow up on your admission status
  • To improve the quality of our counseling and services based on feedback

We will only contact you for matters directly related to your educational enquiry. We do not send unsolicited marketing messages.

🚫

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for commercial or marketing purposes — ever.

Your data may be processed by the following trusted services that are necessary for our operations:

  • Formspree — our form processing service that delivers your enquiry to our email inbox. Formspree is GDPR-compliant and does not use your data for any other purpose.
  • Google Maps — used to display our location on the contact page. No personal data is shared with Google through this embed.
✓ Your information is only ever used to help you with your education. Nothing else.
🗄️

Data Storage & Security

Form submissions are delivered securely to our inbox at contactcoursecampus@gmail.com via Formspree's encrypted connection. We do not maintain a database of your personal information on this website.

Physical records related to your admission (if applicable) are stored securely at our office premises in Sangam Vihar, New Delhi, and are accessible only to authorised staff.

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to assist with your education or as required by applicable law.

⚖️

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal information from our records
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you at any time
  • Ask questions about how your data is being used

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email or phone number below.

🍪

Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes. We do not run any third-party advertising scripts on this site.

Google Maps embeds on our contact page may set functional cookies as part of their normal operation. These are governed by Google's own Privacy Policy.

🔄

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
