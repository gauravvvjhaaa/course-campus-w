About Course Campus

A recognized education centre built on one belief — it is never too late to learn.

⭐ Our Mission

Course Campus helps students restart, recover and rise through NIOS, IGNOU, D.El.Ed and distance university programs.

Founded in Sangam Vihar, New Delhi, we have spent over 9 years turning academic setbacks into success stories. Whether you failed a board exam, dropped out due to personal reasons, or are returning to education after a long gap — we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Why We Started
Course Campus

Course Campus was born from a simple observation: thousands of students in Delhi were failing their boards or dropping out of school — not because they lacked ability, but because they lacked proper guidance and flexible options.

We saw students spending years believing their academic journey was over. We knew it didn't have to be that way. NIOS, IGNOU, and distance programs existed — but most students didn't even know about them.

So we built a centre dedicated entirely to helping those students find their path forward.
