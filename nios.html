Understanding NIOS

What is NIOS?

National Institute of Open Schooling

NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling) is an autonomous organisation under the Ministry of Education, Government of India. It is the world's largest open schooling board, providing flexible and affordable education to students across India.

NIOS certificates carry the same value as CBSE and State Board certificates and are accepted by all universities, government employers, and professional institutions in India.

It is specially designed for students who have failed, dropped out, or couldn't continue regular school due to personal, financial, or health reasons.
